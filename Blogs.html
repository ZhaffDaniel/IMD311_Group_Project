PULAU KAPAS | JT Cafe - Located on the quieter end of the beach of Kapas but worth the walk. Delicious breakfast of eggs roti canai and an evening meal of yellow coconut chicken accompanied with drinks like fresh juices and coffees. During the day you can watch the comings and goings of people on the jetty.

PULAU TIOMAN | Tioman Cabana Beach Bistro - Tioman Cabana Beach Bistro, this bistro cafe concept is highly recommended if you are in Kampung Tekek, Pulau Tioman. Most of the food menu here is based on seafood, Asian and vegetarian including pancakes, omelettes, burgers, rice, noodles and pizza.

PULAU SIPADAN | Summer Breeze Cafe - They have pizza, pasta, local food, lovely desserts with homemade ice cream, you name it! They make you feel like home with the nice furniture and some calm music in the background. This is Sempornas best restaurant compared to other places.

PULAU PANGKOR | Daddy's Cafe - Located on the beach with beautiful views. There is a wide variety of choices on the menu including Malaysian food and different types of Western food like pizza. Highly recommend trying the chicken satay salad & also the Fresh Alu Alu which was cooked to perfection.

PULAU PERHENTIAN | Bayu Restaurant - This restaurant is part of Alunan Resort. The menu provided here consists of local and Western dishes. Here, you may try out Nyonya Rissa Curry Chicken and Rendang Chicken while the Western dishes are Grilled Salmon and Pan Seared Rosemary Chicken.

PULAU MABUL | Ocean Treasure Live Seafood Restaurant - Efficient service, fast preparation of meals even in a busy environment accompanied by calming atmosphere as for the location of the waterfront. Many delicious seafood here to enjoy but the most notable delicacy is the Tiger Shrimp.
