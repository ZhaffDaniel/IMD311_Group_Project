Pulau Mabul - Sabah

Pulau Mabul is an island located off the coast of Sabah, Malaysia. It is known for its exceptional diving and snorkeling opportunities, with clear waters and abundant marine life including exotic fish and sea creatures. The island also offers a laid-back, tropical getaway for tourists, with a range of accommodation options and facilities for visitors. Mabul is easily accessible by boat from nearby cities, making it a popular destination for both experienced and beginner divers.

Interesting activities that can do at Pulau Mabul:

  • Snorkeling
  • Diving
  • Beach Activities
  • Island Exploration
  • Water Sports
  • Cultural Activities
  • Fishing
